# Superstore Sales & Profit Dashboard

Objective:
Analyze Superstore sales and profitability data to identify regional performance trends, category-level insights, and margin optimization opportunities.

Tools Used:
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)

Dashboard Features:
- Interactive slicers for region, category, and time filtering
- Drill-through capability for deeper sales exploration
- Geo-visualization of regional sales performance
- KPI cards tracking Sales, Profit, and Profit Margin

Key Insights:
- Furniture category shows lower margins compared to other segments.
- Western region demonstrates strong revenue concentration.
- Profit variability suggests pricing and discount optimization opportunities.

Business Recommendations:
- Review pricing strategy within Furniture category.
- Strengthen targeted marketing campaigns in high-performing regions.
- Monitor margin trends alongside revenue growth to ensure sustainable profitability.
